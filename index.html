About Me

Hi there! I'm Van, a twenty-something-year-old Northwestern Universty Coding Bootcamp student looking for a new path in life. Previously having graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Statistics and Operations Research (yes, it's as boring as it sounds), I found myself drifting from the life insurance/actuarial industry to an equally exciting career in accounting and operations at an Auntie Anne's franchise located in Skokie, Illinois.

Fast forward three years and twenty pounds of free pretzels later, I decided I wanted more than broken fax machines and killing three million trees a day - so I went on a limb and started to get into coding, which I've been exposed to in the past but never received formal education on. Luckily for me, Northwestern was advertising an affordable, part-time program that I immediately boarded, and, well. Here I am, and I'm excited to start my journey!
